
A - bright yellow, 
E - gold

Maybe useful patches:

B012 Vangelibells, as you'd expect, nice low down
B046 Noise Toys

FX-ee things:

A099 RingThemBells, overwhelming decay, try it with sparse patterns
G128 Bubble World, richly radiophonic (though I don't know how many FX will carry over to multi mode)
H007 EffEmmEffIxx, nasty
H020 Lila Bells, pretty but disturbing
H033 Rise & Fall
H031 RingDaBell, very 80's computer lab sound
H043 The Assault, likewise but moreso
